Network Security

Network security is basically any activities designed to keep your network safe. We will ensure that the usability, reliability, integrity, and safety of your network and data are always protected. With our effective network security, a variety of threats will be prevented from entering or spreading on your network.

 

What Kind of Threats?

The most common threats that lurk throughout the internet include:

  • Viruses, worms, and Trojan horses
  • Spyware and adware
  • Zero-day attacks, aka zero-hour attacks
  • Hacker attacks
  • Denial of service attacks
  • Data interception and theft
  • Identity theft

 

How does it work?

Protection from all of the listed threats requires multiple layers of security. Just in case one fails, there will still be other layers backing you up. Hardware and software are needed for network security. On top of that, it must stay updated and managed to make sure we can stop the latest threats. Dreamtech Computing can provide you with all the software and any equipment you need to properly set up a network security.

A couple of components required for network security include:

  • Anti-virus and anti-spyware
  • Firewall, which can block unauthorized access to your network
  • Intrusion prevention systems (IPS), to identify fast-spreading threats, like zero-day or zero-hour attacks
  • Virtual Private Networks (VPNs), which will provide secure remote access

 

Advantages of Network Security

Having network security can ensure that you and your business will be protected against all threats that can cause business disruption, leading to a more productive workflow. In addition, your company will ultimately meet mandatory regulatory compliance. Not only will your business be protected, but so will your customers’ data.
