Personal Security

If you are looking to protect your own personal computer or device rather than an entire business network, you are in the right place. Everyone with a computer should invest in having personal computer security. The safeguarding of computerized data has increasingly become an integral part of our technological society. Your personal information can be stolen or damaged, outsiders can access your computer without your permission, viruses and worms can come into your system and cause slow-downs and malfunctions, and hackers can hack into your computer. Most computer users are fairly new to these kinds of technology or are not aware of the dangers. Do not panic, Dreamtech Computing will help protect your personal computer!

With our Dreamtech security software, your personal computer will benefit in a number of ways:

  • Anti-virus that can prevent viruses, malware, and other form of threats that can access your computer and cause technical issues. Your computer will be scanned on a regular basis to make sure that no virus is present in your system. You will also have the option to view monthly reports to keep track of any threats that were present and immediately removed.
  • Easily backup and restore any data in case they somehow get erased. Keep them safe in an encrypted folder which gives you the ability to make it password-protected. Your files will be impossible to access.
  • Set up a firewall to help manage traffic. Choose what websites and activities you want to block or allow.
  • Social network protection allows you to safely use social network websites without your personal information being shared without your permission.
  • Links can be scanned to see if they lead to risky web pages.
  • Parental controls allow you to limit software and websites so that your kids may use the computer without the risk of accessing inappropriate websites and content.
  • Other devices can be protected as well such as other computers that you own, tablets, and mobile devices.
